Hi, I'm Jacques

Pro Player & Engineer

I came from Cameroon alone, had no scouting, no offers, no program, lost access to gyms during COVID, and still forced my way into one of the best programs in Canada. I wasn’t given minutes — I earned them by understanding how the system works.

I wasn’t recruited. I didn’t have visibility. I didn’t have guarantees. But I found a way in by emailing coaches and sending men’s league footage. I barely played at first. But I didn’t complain. I adjusted.

By understanding roles, value, and what coaches actually reward, I went from the end of the rotation to conference rookie honors, defensive awards, all-star recognition, multiple championships, medals at the national level and eventually professional basketball — while earning a Civil Engineering Degree.

This guide exists to explain what was necessary, not what was optional.
